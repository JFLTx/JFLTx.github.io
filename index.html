About me

Hi! I am James. Currently a GIS Analyst at Palmer Engineering holding a Bachelor of Science degree from Eastern Kentucky University in Geology and GIS, and a Master of Science degree from the University of Kentucky in Digital Mapping. I have a passion for cartography and spatial analysis, and I am always eager to learn new techniques and tools to create compelling maps that tell stories and inform decision-making.

When I am not haphazardly slamming buttons on my keyboard, I am spending time with my family or doing judo, rucking, or weightlifting. You might catch me on a hike in Kentucky's scenic wilderness!
